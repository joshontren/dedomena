Our Services

Advanced mine surveying
solutions

At Dedomena, we offer comprehensive surveying services tailored to the mining industry's specific needs, delivering precise measurements and valuable insights for your operations.

Opencast Surveys

Comprehensive assessments of open-pit mining sites evaluating geological structures, terrain conditions, and environmental impacts. Essential for pre-mining planning and ongoing operational monitoring, providing accurate spatial data for mine design, excavation planning, safety management, and regulatory compliance.

Topographical Surveys

Detailed mapping of natural and man-made features, including elevation changes, contours, vegetation, structures, and water bodies. These surveys generate precise representations of land surfaces critical for engineering design, construction planning, urban development, and environmental studies.

Stockpile Surveys

Precise measurement of volume, shape, and density of material stored in stockpiles using advanced surveying methods like drone photogrammetry or LiDAR. These surveys provide highly accurate data essential for inventory management, production control, and financial auditing.

Road Surveys

Collection of detailed information about existing or proposed roadways, including alignments, gradients, surface conditions, widths, and surrounding features. These surveys are crucial for designing new roads, planning maintenance, and ensuring safety standards in transportation infrastructure.

Volumetric Surveys

Calculation of material volumes such as soil, rock, or waste within defined areas. These precise measurements are vital for earthworks, stockpile management, mining operations, and construction projects, assisting in budgeting, resource planning, and ensuring project milestones are met efficiently.

Rehabilitation Surveys & Monitoring

Assessment of land restoration progress after mining or construction activities, tracking changes in vegetation cover, soil stability, water quality, and ecosystem health over time. These surveys guide land management strategies, ensure regulatory compliance, and support sustainable development goals.

Borehole Surveys & Stakeouts

Examination of subsurface conditions via drilled holes, providing critical geological and geotechnical information. Stakeouts involve marking precise locations on the ground to guide the placement of structures or excavation based on design plans, ensuring accurate alignment and efficiency.

Exploration Surveys

Identification and assessment of natural resources potential through various methods including geophysical, geochemical, and remote sensing techniques. These surveys gather data to inform decision-making for further exploration, drilling, or preservation, determining the feasibility of future extraction or conservation efforts.

Our Technology

Cutting-edge equipment
for precise measurements

At Dedomena, we utilize the most advanced surveying technology in the industry to deliver unmatched accuracy and efficiency in our mine surveys.

High-Precision Drones

Our fleet of top-range drones equipped with LiDAR and RTK technology provides centimeter-level accuracy for surveying even the most challenging terrain.

  • Aerial Mapping Precision
  • High-Resolution Imaging
  • Advanced Flight Technology

Advanced Modeling Software

We use industry-leading software to transform survey data into highly detailed 3D models and volumetric calculations with exceptional accuracy.

  • Terrain Modelling Software
  • Volumetric Analysis Tool
  • CAD-Based Survey Processing

Total Station Systems

Our Trimble total station technology delivers sub-millimeter precision and reliability, enabling accurate data capture in even the harshest mining conditions. Its advanced optics and integrated systems ensure consistent performance for both opencast and underground surveys.

  • High-Precision Measurement
  • Laser Distance Accuracy
  • Integrated Surveying Solutions

GPS/GNSS Systems

Trimble GNSS receivers utilize multi-constellation satellite tracking to deliver millimeter-level accuracy for boundary surveys, control networks, and high-precision positioning in mining environments. Their robust design ensures consistent performance even under challenging field conditions.

  • Multi-Constellation GNSS
