About us

Mine surveying expertise
you can trust

Dedomena is a specialized mine surveying company based in South Africa. We excel in calculating volumes for pits, stockpiles, and any mining-related terrain with exceptional precision.

Our team of expert surveyors utilizes cutting-edge technology including top-range drones and advanced surveying equipment to deliver the most accurate data and measurements for mining operations of all sizes.

Pit Calculations

Our Technology

Cutting-edge equipment
for precise measurements

At Dedomena, we utilize the most advanced surveying technology in the industry to deliver unmatched accuracy and efficiency in our mine surveys.

High-Precision Drones

Our fleet of top-range drones equipped with LiDAR and RTK technology provides centimeter-level accuracy for surveying even the most challenging terrain.

  • 30MP Camera Resolution
  • RTK GPS Precision
  • 45-minute Flight Time

Advanced Modeling Software

We use industry-leading software to transform survey data into highly detailed 3D models and volumetric calculations with exceptional accuracy.

  • Real-time Data Processing
  • Sub-centimeter Precision
  • Advanced Terrain Analysis

Total Station Systems

Our industry-leading total station equipment allows us to capture extremely precise measurements even in the most challenging mine environments.

  • 0.5" Angular Accuracy
  • Auto-targeting Technology
  • All-weather Operation

GPS/GNSS Systems

Multi-constellation GNSS receivers provide millimeter-level accuracy for boundary surveys and control networks in mining operations.

  • Multi-frequency Tracking
  • RTK Positioning
  • Rugged Mining Design
